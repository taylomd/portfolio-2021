Some of my favorites

The picture on the left was taken on Lakeshore Drive in Chicago, IL last summer. Although I’ve visited Chicago a lot as a kid, this trip was really fun because I got to go with friends and stay at their house. If you look directly in the center, you can see the John Hancock center, which is hub for tourists, shopping, and businesses. It is also where you can see the “Skydeck” of Chicago!

The picture on the right is a snapshot of the first digital product I ever worked on. It’s called Global Taste, and it was made over winter break of this year during a bootcamp course I completed. Working with a global, remote team, we decided to make an app that encompasses all walks of culture in food. We also focused a lot on language and culture accessibility, which is something we kept in mind the whole time while designing, researching, and iterating.
