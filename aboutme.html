About Me

Scroll down to learn a little more about me!

I'm currently a second-year student looking to focus in UX Design at the University of Michigan. I have coursework in psychology, statistics, and data programming, and I am currently taking courses in web development, digital product design, and entrepreneurial studies.

I am also an incoming UX Design intern at Quicken Loans for this summer! Outside of design, my professional interests lie in product management, financial technology, and digital media.

I come from a small town and graduated from Potterville High School (MI) in 2019. In my free time, I like to keep up with current events, watch sports and Netflix, and read science fiction and psychology books.
